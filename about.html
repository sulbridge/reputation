Our Story

Founded by Arsenii Vonh, a former digital strategist, after watching a family‑owned business nearly collapse from 3 fake reviews that AI search tools amplified — costing them over $200k in lost bookings. We built ReputationGuard.pro to give businesses a fair fight in the AI‑powered review economy.

Today, we’re a remote‑first team serving clients from Texas to Toronto — and we treat every client’s reputation like it’s our own.

Our Mission

To make sure every business gets the AI visibility they deserve. We believe that fake reviews should never stand between you and your customers. By cleaning your reputation data, we enable ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI to recommend your brand with confidence.

What We Do
