Our Story

We didn't discover AI visibility yesterday. We've spent almost two decades learning how brands earn trust online, first from search engines, now from AI.

2008

Our first website, for a supply business

It started with a single hand‑coded HTML site built for a supply‑chain business. No frameworks, no shortcuts, just learning, line by line, what makes a business believable online.

2009 – 2018

The SEO decade

That first site turned into hundreds more. We moved deep into SEO, built and grew dozens of technology and internet businesses, and spent a decade figuring out exactly what search engines, and the people behind them, trust and reward.

2019 – 2022

The rise of neural networks

As the first large language models emerged, we saw the shift coming early: the gatekeeper to customers was changing from search rankings to AI answers. We started studying how these models form opinions about brands.

Today

AI recommendations, automated at scale

Now most of the process is automated: continuous audits, signal cleanup, and monitoring across ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude and Perplexity. That's 18 years of hands‑on experience, from one HTML page to a full AI Visibility Engine, distilled into a system built to get you recommended.
