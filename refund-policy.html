Refund Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

At ReputationGuard, we stand behind our services. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued for our One‑Time Service and Subscription Plans.

1. One‑Time Service ($99 Review Removal)

  • Full refund if we fail: If we are unable to remove the selected Google review, we will issue a full refund of your $99 payment within 5 business days of our final determination.
  • No refund if successful: If the review is removed, the fee is earned and non‑refundable.
  • No partial refunds: Because we invest significant manual effort (analysis, evidence collection, appeal preparation) before filing, we do not offer pro‑rata refunds once work has commenced.

2. Subscription Plans (Essential, Pro, Enterprise)

  • Pilot Period (first month): You may cancel at the end of the Pilot without further obligation. No refund is issued during the Pilot – you are paying for the service received that month.
  • Minimum Commitment (months 2–4): After the Pilot (or immediately if you skip it), you are committed to 3 consecutive monthly payments. No refunds are available during the Minimum Commitment, even if you cancel early – you remain liable for the full commitment.
  • After the Minimum Commitment (month‑to‑month): You may cancel with 30 days’ written notice. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No refund is issued for the remaining days of that month – the service runs until the end of the period.
  • Unsuccessful appeals: Because Google makes the final decision, we do not refund subscription fees if individual appeals are rejected. Our service includes ongoing monitoring and multiple attempts, and we continue working on your behalf throughout the subscription.

3. What Does NOT Qualify for a Refund

  • Unsuccessful appeals (for both One‑Time and Subscriptions) – we do not guarantee removal.
  • Change of mind – if you simply decide you no longer want the service after work has begun.
  • Delays caused by you – failure to grant “Manager” access or provide information in a timely manner.
  • Duplicate or erroneous charges – these will be refunded (see Section 4).

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at onboard@reputationguard.pro within 14 days of the charge. Include:

  • Your full name and business name
  • The invoice number or transaction ID (found in your Stripe receipt)
  • A clear reason for the request (e.g., “One‑Time Service – review not removed”)

We will respond within 5 business days. If a refund is approved, we will process it to your original payment method within 7 business days.

5. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank without first contacting us, we reserve the right to terminate your account and pursue collection of any outstanding fees (including the Minimum Commitment if applicable). We also reserve the right to dispute chargebacks with appropriate evidence of service delivery.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting with a revised “Effective date”. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes your acceptance.
