Refund Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

At ReputationGuard, we are committed to transparency and fair dealing. Because our services involve significant manual effort (review analysis, evidence collection, and appeal filing), our refund policy is as follows:

1. Monthly Subscription Fees

Monthly subscription fees are generally non‑refundable. Once an appeal has been filed (or a review analysis has been performed), the work has been done and no refund will be issued for that billing period.

2. When a Refund May Be Issued

We will consider a refund on a case‑by‑case basis only if:

  • No appeal was filed: If during the billing cycle we have not filed any appeal on your behalf (due to technical error on our side or because you provided no eligible reviews), you may request a refund for that month.
  • Duplicate or erroneous charge: If you were accidentally charged twice or for a plan you did not select, we will refund the excess amount.
  • Service not performed: If we fail to perform the agreed‑upon services (monitoring or appeals) for an entire billing cycle without justification, a pro‑rata refund may be issued.

3. What Does NOT Qualify for a Refund

  • Unsuccessful appeals: Because Google has the final authority over review removal, we cannot guarantee success. Refunds are not provided if an appeal is rejected by Google.
  • Change of mind or cancellation mid‑month: If you cancel your subscription before the end of a billing cycle, no refund for that cycle will be given, but you will not be billed for future months.
  • Delays caused by you: If you fail to provide necessary access or information, resulting in delayed or missed appeals, no refund will be issued.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at defend@reputationguard.pro within 14 days of the charge. Include your business name, the invoice number, and a clear explanation of why you believe a refund is due. We will respond within 5 business days.

5. Overage Fees

Overage fees (additional appeals beyond your plan limit) are non‑refundable once the appeal has been filed.

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us to resolve a dispute, we reserve the right to terminate your account and pursue collection of any outstanding fees.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes your acceptance.

8. Contact for Refund Questions

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us before making a payment. We are happy to clarify our policies.
