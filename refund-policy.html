1. AI Visibility Boost ($249 / One‑Time)

  • Full refund if we fail: If we are unable to complete the full 9‑layer AI visibility audit and deliver the 3 critical signal boosts as outlined in the service description, we will issue a full refund of your $249 payment within 5 business days.
  • No refund if successful: If we complete the audit and boosts as described, the fee is earned and non‑refundable.
  • No partial refunds: Because we invest significant manual effort (analysis, evidence collection, boost implementation) before delivery, we do not offer pro‑rata refunds once work has commenced.

2. Subscription Plans (AI Visibility, AI Authority, AI Dominate)

  • Pilot Period (first month): You may cancel at the end of the Pilot without further obligation. No refund is issued during the Pilot – you are paying for the service received that month.
  • Minimum Commitment (months 2–4): After the Pilot (or immediately if you skip it), you are committed to 3 consecutive monthly payments. No refunds are available during the Minimum Commitment, even if you cancel early – you remain liable for the full commitment. This applies regardless of whether the Visibility Score Improvement Guarantee in Section 2a has been triggered.
  • After the Minimum Commitment (month‑to‑month): You may cancel with 30 days' written notice. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No refund is issued for the remaining days of that month – the service runs until the end of the period.
  • Overage fees: If you exceed your plan's fair use limit (Boosts), additional charges apply. These overage fees are non‑refundable once processed, as they correspond to work already performed.
  • Unsuccessful boosts: Because AI platforms make independent decisions, we do not refund subscription fees if individual Boosts do not produce the desired outcome on a specific AI platform. Our service includes ongoing monitoring and continuous improvement throughout the subscription. See Section 2a for the separate AI Visibility Score guarantee, which applies to your overall score rather than to individual platform outcomes.

2a. Visibility Score Improvement Guarantee

All subscription plans (AI Visibility, AI Authority, AI Dominate) include a Visibility Score Improvement Guarantee, as advertised on our pricing page. This section defines exactly what that guarantee covers and how it interacts with the Minimum Commitment described in Section 2.

What is guaranteed:

  • We guarantee an improvement in your AI Visibility Score (our internal 0–100 metric, described in our methodology) within the guarantee window specific to your plan: 60 days (AI Visibility), 30 days (AI Authority), or 21 days (AI Dominate).
  • This guarantee applies only to the AI Visibility Score itself. It does not guarantee a specific ranking, placement, or mention frequency on any individual AI platform (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, etc.), as these are determined independently by third parties outside our control.

What "we keep working for free" means: If your AI Visibility Score has not improved by the end of your plan's guarantee window, we will continue performing additional Boosts at no extra charge until your score improves. This does not pause, discount, or refund your regular subscription payments – your subscription continues to bill according to its normal schedule, including any Pilot Period or Minimum Commitment obligations described in Section 2. "Free" refers specifically to the additional Boost work performed beyond your plan's monthly Boost allowance, not to your subscription fee.

This guarantee does not create an exception to the Minimum Commitment or Pilot Period terms in Section 2. If you cancel during the Minimum Commitment period, you remain liable for the full commitment regardless of whether the guarantee has been triggered.

3. What Does NOT Qualify for a Refund

  • Unsuccessful boosts or audit outcomes (for both One‑Time and Subscriptions) – we do not guarantee specific AI rankings.
  • Change of mind – if you simply decide you no longer want the service after work has begun.
  • Delays caused by you – failure to grant necessary access or provide information in a timely manner.
  • Duplicate or erroneous charges – these will be refunded (see Section 4).

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at defend@reputationguard.pro within 14 days of the charge. Include:

  • Your full name and business name
  • The invoice number or transaction ID (found in your Stripe receipt)
  • A clear reason for the request (e.g., "AI Visibility Boost – audit not completed")

We will respond within 5 business days. If a refund is approved, we will process it to your original payment method within 7 business days.

Note: To cancel your subscription or update your payment method, please use the Stripe Customer Portal – no email required for these actions.

5. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank without first contacting us, we reserve the right to terminate your account and pursue collection of any outstanding fees (including the Minimum Commitment if applicable). We also reserve the right to dispute chargebacks with appropriate evidence of service delivery.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting with a revised "Effective date". Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes your acceptance.

7. Contact for Refund Questions

If you have any questions about this policy before purchasing, please contact us – we are happy to clarify.
