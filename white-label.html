Frequently Asked Questions

How does white‑label work exactly?
You forward client review removal requests to us. We handle everything – evidence, appeals, follow‑up – and deliver results in a report that carries your logo and your brand name. Your client never knows we exist.
What if a review cannot be removed?
We never guarantee removal, but we maximize success. In cases where removal is impossible, we provide a detailed explanation and suggest alternative strategies (e.g., suppression). You decide how to communicate this to your client.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No. You can start with a one‑time removal or monthly retainer. Cancel anytime – no lock‑in contracts.
Can I resell your service to my existing clients?
Absolutely. That's the whole point. You set your own retail price. We charge the client directly and pay you a commission on every sale.
How do I track progress and results?
You get access to a white‑label dashboard where you can monitor all submitted reviews, appeal status, and completed removals. You also receive weekly email summaries.
How do I get paid?
We pay commissions via international bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA) in USD. Net-15 terms after the client's payment is confirmed.
