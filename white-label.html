Frequently Asked Questions

Do I set my own prices?
Yes. You buy at wholesale, you set your own retail price. We never contact your clients.
What if a reputation signal cannot be fixed?
We never guarantee 100% success, but we maximize results. In cases where a signal is impossible to clean, we provide a detailed explanation and suggest alternative strategies (e.g., suppression). You decide how to communicate this to your client.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No. You can start with a one‑time audit or monthly retainer. Cancel anytime – no lock‑in contracts.
How do I get paid by my client?
You invoice your client directly. We invoice you for wholesale. You keep the difference. The financial flow is fully independent.
How do I track progress?
You get access to a white‑label dashboard where you can monitor all submitted clients, signal status, and completed boosts.
Do I need to sign a contract?
Yes. We sign a formal reseller agreement that outlines wholesale pricing, territory rights, confidentiality, and payment terms.
