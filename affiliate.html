Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my commissions?
During the application process, you'll specify the countries or cities where you operate. All clients coming from those territories will be automatically attributed to you in our CRM. We send you a detailed monthly report via email showing all clients from your territory, their active subscriptions, and your commission amount. Full transparency — no surprises.
When are commissions paid and how?
All commissions are paid Net-15 after the client's payment is confirmed. Payments are made via international bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA) directly to your bank account in USD. We sign a formal agreement before you start.
Is there a minimum payout threshold?
No minimum. We pay you the full amount due every month, no matter how small.
Do you provide marketing materials?
Yes. You'll get access to banners, email templates, case studies, and sales scripts to help you close deals faster.
Can I combine this with my own agency?
Absolutely. Many of our partners are agencies that white‑label our services or refer clients for a commission. Your agency and our partnership can coexist seamlessly.
Is my territory exclusive?
Yes. Once your territory is approved, all clients from that region are exclusively attributed to you. No other ReputationGuard partner will operate in your designated area.
Do I need to sign a contract?
Yes. We sign a formal partnership agreement that outlines commission structures, territory rights, confidentiality, and payment terms. This protects both parties and ensures clarity.
