How We Measure AI Visibility

How We Measure AI Visibility

Our 8‑Layer Analysis framework. Each layer represents a critical signal AI systems use to understand and recommend your brand.

L1 Access

Can AI crawlers reach your website? If blocked, buyers get answers from competitors.

L2 Structure

Can AI read your content structure? Messy pages = messy AI answers.

L3 Authority

Which page is your official source? Confusing signals split AI trust.

L4 Clarity

Is your offer clear? Vague copy creates vague AI answers.

L5 Trust Signals

Can your site be trusted? Weak signals reduce AI confidence.

L6 Packaging

Is your message packaged with schema? Poor structure makes you harder to explain.

L7 Citation Readiness

Are you cite-ready? Low readiness means AI may avoid citing you.

L8 Activity

Do you look active? Stale signals reduce urgency and trust.

L9 ★ Reputation Signal critical

Does your brand have clean, verified reviews? AI models read review sentiment as a primary trust signal. Fake or negative reviews tell AI to avoid recommending you. Clean reviews = AI trust.
